Chef's Presentation

Toasted English Muffin topped with our house-made Lonza, finished with two perfectly poached eggs drizzled in our rich, velvety béarnaise sauce. Served alongside a fresh, crisp petite lettuce salad, dressed in a light lemon vinaigrette.

Allergies

Egg - Pouched Eggs, and Eggs in Béarnaise Sauce

Gluten - English Muffin

Dairy - Butter in Béarnaise Sauce

Pork - Lonza

Nightshade - Pepper found in our Lonza and Béarnaise Sauce

Allium - Shallots in Béarnaise Sauce

Citrus - Lemon in Lemon Vinaigrette Salad Dressing (Removable)

Sulfite - Sulfites are found in White Wine Vinaigrette, which we in our Béarnaise Sauce. (Sulfites are common preservatives found in White Wines, Sparkling Wines, and some Red Wines)

Béarnaise Sauce

White Whine Vinegar Reduction with:

• Black peppercorns

• Tarragon

• Shallots

The base is then emulsified with:

• Egg Yolks

• Butter

• Dijon Mustard

The mixture is transferred to an ISI dispenser, charged with N2O three times, creating a light, airy sauce that perfectly clings to the poached eggs.

Lonza

Our pork loin is Brined for Five Days, then coated with a signature Spice Blend and roasted to a perfect medium. The tender loin is sliced thin, with two 1.5 oz portions served per order.

House Spice Blend

• Mustard Seeds

• Peppercorn
