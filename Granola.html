Allergies

Dairy - Buttermilk in CocoJune Yougurt

Gluten - Rolled Oats ( Cross Contamination in Factory ), also, Oats contain a protein called Avenin, which is similar to Gluten. Even if the Oats are labeled Gluten-Free, Avenin is still present

Tree Nuts - Pecans, Coconut ( The FDA classifies Coconut as a Tree Nut Allergen, making it similar to pecans in terms of allergy risk ) present in our Granola and Yogurt, respectively

Seeds - Sesame Seeds, Sunflower Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds, Flax Seeds, and Chia Seeds in our Granola

Citrus - Orange Zest in our Granola and Lemon Juice in Raspberry Jam

Bee Related - Honey in Granola

Sulfites - Brown Sugar in Granola, ( Sulfite is a Preservative often found in Wines and Sugars )
