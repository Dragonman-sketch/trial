Chef's Presentation

Creamy Mashed Avocado is seasoned with Chardonnay Vinegar, Extra Virgin Olive Oil

( EVOO ), Fresh Lime Juice, Cilantro, and Chives. We spread the Avocado generously over Toasted Seeded Bread and top it with Salsa Criolla, Crumbled Queso Fresco, and thinly Sliced Radishes.

Guests can choose to add Any Style of Egg to their Toast for an additional $4.
