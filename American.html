Allergies

Egg - Happy Co Eggs fried in Glarified Butter

Dairy - Clarified Butter to fry Eggs ( Replace with Olive Oil )

Glutten - Cross Contamination in Breakfast Potatoes and Toasts ( Recommend Gluten-Free Toast )

Allium - Garlic and Onion in Sofrito Mix from Breakfast Potatoes ( Removable ), Chives in Salmon from Selection of Protein

Nightshade - Breakfast Potatoes and Green Bell Peppers in Sofrito ( Substitute )

Pork - Bacon or Pork Sausage from Selection of Protein ( Recommend Turkey Bacon or Chicken Sausage)

Fish - Smoked Salmon from Selection of Protein

Sesame - Sesame Seeds finish our Smoked Salmon

Please note: Our Sofrito Mix contains Allium, so it is not suitable for individuals with an Allium Allergy. Additionally, Breakfast Potatoes are a Nightshade Vegetable, and should be avoided by anyone with a Nightshade Sensitivity.
